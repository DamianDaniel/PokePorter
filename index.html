FAQ

Is this official guidance?

No. This site is a playful, educational visualization. Steps can vary by game/version/region and may change over time. Always cross‑check with official documentation.

What are the rules of moving Pokémon forward?
  • Once moved to modern services, transfers are typically one‑way.
  • Some Pokémon, forms, moves, ribbons, or origin marks can change or be incompatible.
  • You’ll often need subscriptions or apps like Pokémon Bank, Poké Transporter, and Pokémon HOME.
Accessibility

The UI is keyboard navigable. All interactive elements have ARIA labels, and animations respect reduced‑motion settings.
